MOON VIBES 

Moon

The moon shines in the quiet night,
A gentle, calm, and silver light.
It smiles above the trees so high,
Like a peaceful lamp in the sky.

It watches rivers, hills, and seas,
And dances softly with the breeze.
It gives us hope when days are done,
A quiet friend for everyone.

The stars sparkle all around,
Making magic without a sound.
The moon reminds us every day,
That light can always find its way.
